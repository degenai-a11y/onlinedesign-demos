Our Story

A Saugus Landmark

A wonderful old building with a rich history and a warm community.

The M.E.G. Foundation is a beloved community hall located at 58 Essex Street in Saugus, Massachusetts. With its historic charm and spacious rooms, it has been the home of countless celebrations, community events, and gatherings.

Whether you're planning a family event, a community fundraiser, or a seasonal celebration, the M.E.G. Foundation provides a welcoming space with character you won't find anywhere else.
